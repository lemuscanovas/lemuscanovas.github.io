About Me

I am a pre-doctoral researcher at the Climatology Group of the University of Barcelona, where I am working on my PhD thesis on the characterization of precipitation and climate change in mountain areas, specifically in the Pyrenees. Currently my efforts are focused on obtaining high resolution climate projections in this area. Apart from my interest in climate, I am also interested in everything around R programming, since practically anything imaginable with data is possible with R. This interest has led me to enroll recently in a BSc in Applied Data Science at the Open University of Catalonia!

The aim of this site is to share “tips” about R, mainly for climate analysis. For any question, do not hesitate to contact me!
